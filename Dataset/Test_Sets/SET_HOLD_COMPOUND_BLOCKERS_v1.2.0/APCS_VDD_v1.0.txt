----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Executive summary
----------------------------------------------------------------

The v1.2.0 promotion gate cannot be cleared. Multiple compounding items
are open simultaneously:

  (a) An unresolved runtime blocker on T-APCS-004 (incorrect occupancy
      values under rapid updates), of the same failure family as
      BLK-2026-017 from v1.1.2.
  (b) Continuity from the previous gate is NOT closed; no formal closure
      evidence has been attached to BLK-2026-017.
  (c) The integration-layer fix exists only as a local workaround on the
      QA test bench; no new official version has been generated.
  (d) T-APCS-006 is in pending validation and was not re-executed on
      build.4222.

Any one of these items would justify a HOLD; together they make the
release unambiguously not ready.

4. Continuity & governance
----------------------------------------------------------------

The continuity gate is NOT closed. The new T-APCS-004 finding on
build.4222 reactivates the same failure family that was supposed to be
closed by the v1.1.2 hotfix. Until continuity closure is explicit and
supported by a re-test of T-APCS-004 on a new official build, the gate
cannot be cleared.

----------------------------------------------------------------
5. Risks
----------------------------------------------------------------

  - Functional reliability: incorrect occupancy values would propagate
    to consumers of the integration-layer, with downstream impact on
    operator dashboards and analytics.
  - Evidence integrity: relying on a test-bench-only workaround would
    create a documented gap between the validated state and the shipped
    state.
  - Governance debt: shipping with continuity not closed accumulates
    Agent4 hard blockers across releases.

----------------------------------------------------------------
6. Recommendation
----------------------------------------------------------------

HOLD. Required actions before re-evaluation:

  1. Generate a new official version of the integration-layer that
     incorporates the ordering fix.
  2. Re-execute T-APCS-004, T-APCS-005, T-APCS-006, T-APCS-010 on the
     new build.
  3. Attach explicit closure evidence to BLK-2026-017, countersigned by
     Backend Tech Lead and QA Lead.
  4. Re-issue this VDD against the new build.

Do not proceed to TEST in the current state.
